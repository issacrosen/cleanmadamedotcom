Cancellation Policy

We require at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations, suspensions, or postponements. A $25 cancellation fee applies to cancellations made within 24 hours of your scheduled booking.

Please ensure you can provide unencumbered access to your property at the scheduled time. Failure to provide access will result in automatic booking termination and a $25 administrative fee.
