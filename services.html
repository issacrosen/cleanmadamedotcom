Booking & Cancellation Policies

Important information about our service policies

Booking Requirements

  • All bookings require a 3-hour minimum
  • Payment is due at the time of booking
  • All areas to be cleaned must be specified in the booking request
  • Access to the property must be provided to the cleaning providers
  • Any hazards, special conditions, or challenging areas must be disclosed when booking

Cancellation Policy

24-Hour Notice Required: You must provide at least 24 hours' notice to cancel, suspend, or postpone your booking.

Late Cancellation Fee: Cancellations within 24 hours of the scheduled booking will incur a $25 cancellation fee to cover administrative and travel costs.

Access Requirements

Property Access: If unencumbered access to the booking location is not provided for the cleaning providers, the booking will be automatically terminated and a $25 cancellation fee will be charged.

Changes to Bookings

  • No changes may be made to bookings except with approval from Clean Madame LLC via our platform
  • Cleaning providers are not authorized to agree to changes directly
  • All booking modifications must be requested through our customer service team

Meal Time Breaks

If a booking overlaps with a meal time, cleaning providers are entitled to a 15-minute break. While not required, we recommend providing food and drink during meal times.
