What to Expect

Everything you need to know before your first cleaning

Before the Cleaning

  • You'll receive confirmation with provider details and scheduled time
  • Ensure all areas to be cleaned are accessible
  • Secure any valuables or personal items you prefer not to be moved
  • Provide access to the property (key, code, or be present)
  • Note any specific instructions or areas requiring special attention

During the Cleaning

  • Cleaning providers arrive with professional supplies and equipment
  • They'll clean all specified areas according to your booking
  • You can be present or away - whatever you prefer
  • If the booking overlaps with meal time, providers take a 15-minute break
  • All work is performed by background-checked professionals

After the Cleaning

  • Inspect the cleaned areas to ensure satisfaction
  • Provide feedback about your experience
  • Schedule your next cleaning if you'd like recurring service
  • Request the same provider for future bookings if you were satisfied

Common Questions

How far in advance do I need to book? +
We recommend booking as early as possible to ensure availability, especially for specific dates or times. However, we often have providers available on short notice. Contact us to check current availability.
Do I need to be home during the cleaning? +
No, you don't need to be present. Many customers provide access via a key, lockbox code, or building concierge. Just ensure the cleaning provider can access all areas that need to be cleaned.
What if I'm not satisfied with the cleaning? +
Your satisfaction is important to us. If you have concerns about the service, please contact us immediately so we can address the issue. We strive to ensure every customer is happy with their cleaning experience.
Can I set up recurring weekly or monthly cleanings? +
Absolutely! We offer flexible recurring service options including weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly schedules. We'll do our best to assign the same cleaning provider for consistency.
