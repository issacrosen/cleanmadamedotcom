Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our cleaning services

How do I book a cleaning service? +
You can book in three convenient ways: call us at (845) 212-4444, send us a text message, or fill out our online booking form on the Contact page. We'll match you with an available cleaning provider based on your preferred date, time, and location.
What is your cancellation policy? +
We require at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations. If you cancel within 24 hours of your scheduled booking, a $25 cancellation fee will apply to cover administrative and travel costs.
Are your cleaning providers insured and background-checked? +
Yes! All cleaning providers on our platform are thoroughly screened and background-checked. We invest significant resources in identifying and vetting professional cleaning providers to ensure your safety and satisfaction.
What areas do you service? +
We serve the entire NYC tri-state area, including all five boroughs of New York City, parts of New Jersey (including Jersey City, Newark, and Hoboken), and Connecticut (Fairfield County). Contact us to confirm service availability in your specific area.
Can I request the same cleaning provider for future bookings? +
Yes! We make our best efforts to accommodate requests for the same cleaning provider for subsequent bookings. While we cannot guarantee availability, we understand the value of consistency and will do our best to match you with your preferred provider.
Do I need to provide cleaning supplies? +
Our cleaning providers come equipped with professional cleaning supplies and equipment. However, if you have specific products you'd like us to use or particular areas that require special attention, please let us know when booking.
